Find the Verb in the Sentence

Section 1

Read each sentence and find the verb (or verbs).

  1. 1
    She plays tennis every Saturday.
    Answer: plays
  2. 2
    They are watching a movie right now.
    Answer: are watching
  3. 3
    She has finished her homework already.
    Answer: has finished
  4. 4
    The cake was made by my sister.
    Answer: was made
  5. 5
    If it rains tomorrow, we will stay at home.
    Answer: rains, will stay
  6. 6
    I enjoy reading books in my free time.
    Answer: enjoy, reading
  7. 7
    Not only did she win the competition, but she also broke the record.
    Answer: did win, broke
  8. 8
    Had I known about the problem, I would have acted differently.
    Answer: had known, would have acted

Writing Prompts

Section 5

Choose one prompt and write 2 paragraphs.

1

What is your daily routine?

2

Describe a memorable trip.

3

What are the advantages and disadvantages of social media?

4

Do you believe technology is making it more difficult for people to interact face-to-face? Explain your answer.

💡 Writing Checklist — Try to include:

  • A variety of tenses (past, present, future, perfect)
  • Conditional sentences (if/would/could)
  • Both passive and active voice
  • Relative clauses (who, which, that)
  • Technical or topic-specific vocabulary
  • 2 full paragraphs
